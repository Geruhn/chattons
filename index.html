Welcome to Chattons

This addon to twitch is supposed to help with sending customizable texts to a twitch chat of your liking. Like "!bet 400" if you want to bet 400 of the chat currency on anything.


Your settings will be saved to the local storage of your browser, so if you already connected once with this device and browser just hit connect!




To get your OAuth-Token use twitchapps.com/tmi.
